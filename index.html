Cultures

South Africa is a melting pot of cultures with a rich history and diverse traditions.

  • Zulu Culture: Known for their warrior traditions and vibrant ceremonies.
  • Xhosa Culture: Famous for their intricate beadwork and music.
  • Afro-European Culture: A blend of African and European influences.

Meals

Bobotie

A traditional South African dish made with spiced minced meat and an egg-based topping.

Biltong

A form of dried, cured meat that is a popular snack in South Africa.

Malva Pudding

A sweet pudding of Dutch origin, usually served hot with custard or ice-cream.
